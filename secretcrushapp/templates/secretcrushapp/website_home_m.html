What is Hidento?

Hidento is a dating site for people who wish to keep their dating life totally private. Most people are reluctant to create a public dating profile. They don’t want everyone to know that they are open for dating. At Hidento, nobody can see anybody’s account. Nobody will even know you have an account in Hidento.

Then how does this work? Using Instagram usernames. You can connect your Hidento account with your Instagram username. Your friends and followers can send dating referrals to you using your Instagram username. All referrals are anonymous. If you like any of the referrals received, you can add them to your Interests list. Hidento will match two people if they are interested in each other. You can also add someone as your Interest directly. This will help you find if your secret crush is interested in you.

No membership fees. No problem of fake accounts. Happy dating at Hidento!
