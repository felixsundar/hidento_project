About Us

There are tonnes of dating sites around the world. Most of them require costly membership plans and they are flooded with fake accounts. The chances of finding a match in those sites are minuscule.

Hidento aims to solve this problem by bringing back the age old practice of REFERRALS into the modern scenario. Hidento enables users to anonymously send and receive dating referrals from their friends and social circle using their Instagram usernames. Hidento does not require paid membership. And a fake account in Hidento will be of no use since Hidento accounts are not visible to anyone. All that users need to know is the Instagram usernames of their friends.

Hidento is the effort a Bangalore based software engineer.
